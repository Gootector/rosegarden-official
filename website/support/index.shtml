Support

If you need assistance in installing, using or optimising Rosegarden, or with other software that works with it, there are several places you can turn.

Community

Rosegarden has an active developer and user community, and there are also numerous resources available for Linux audio and music software users in general.

  • Rosegarden mailing lists – your first port of call for discussion about Rosegarden.
  • Rosegarden SourceForge page, home of the Rosegarden bug tracking and feature request systems.
  • Linux Audio Users and Linux Audio Developers mailing lists.

Commercial Installation and Development Support

Rosegarden and Linux audio in general make a tremendously attractive proposition for education and small studio environments.

Check out some of the support options for such environments.
