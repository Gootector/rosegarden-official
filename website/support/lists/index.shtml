Mailing lists

Much discussion about using, developing and generally living with Rosegarden goes on in our two mailing lists.

  • For any discussion about installing or using Rosegarden or about music made with Rosegarden, please use the rosegarden-user mailing list. Check out the rosegarden-user archives too. If you're concerned about a problem you're having with Rosegarden, make sure you check the Frequently Asked Questions page as well.
  • If you're a developer interested in finding out more about the development of our project and perhaps even joining in, the rosegarden-devel mailing list is for you. There's usually some lively discussion going on.
