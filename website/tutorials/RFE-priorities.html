Feature Request Priorities

Feature Requests should be submitted with a default priority of 5. A new priority will be assigned once someone looks at the idea and considers how likely it is to be implemented in the foreseeable future. (Please bear in mind that “foreseeable” could still be a long, long time, and sometimes it never gets here at all.)
