Rosegarden at the Linux Expo 2003

London, 8th and 9th October 2003

Rosegarden had a corner in the .ORG Village of the Linux Expo, where we set up a few noisy bits of kit, gave a few demos, played some tea-time medleys, talked to a lot of people and did some useful testing toward the 0.9.2 release.
