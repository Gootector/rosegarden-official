Tour 5: Language support

Rosegarden is an international project with contributors all over the world, and one way that people can contribute is by providing and maintaining translations of the GUI into other languages than its original English.



Rosegarden is available in nine languages so far, as well as American and British English. And it's all thanks to the hard work of...

Alexandre Prokoudine – Russian
Pedro Lopez-Cabanillas – Spanish
Jörg Schumann – German
Kevin Donnelly – Welsh
Didier Burli – French
Daniele Medri – Italian
Stefan Asserhäll – Swedish
Hasso Tepper – Estonian

Is your language on that list? If not, perhaps you or somebody you know might be able to maintain a translation.
