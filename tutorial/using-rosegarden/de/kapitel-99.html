10 User Projects

Have you done something with Rosegarden? Would you like to share your process of creation with the rest of the users?

I'm looking for people to create short, self-contained mini-tutorials describing how they used Rosegarden's features to accomplish something interesting. This whole document has thus far been strongly colored by my own experiences, and my own preferences. Everyone approaches a piece of software differently, and I think it would be great to feature a variety of different ways of doing real work here.

This concept isn't fully-formed, and I haven't yet decided how best to integrate them within the framework of Using Rosegarden. Thus far, I haven't had to make any decisions because no one has been forthcoming with any material for me to work in.
