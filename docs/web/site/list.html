The Rosegarden mailing-list

To join the Rosegarden mailing-list, just send an email to rosegarden-subscribe@lists.bath.ac.uk. Your message does not need any substance. (Note that this address has changed recently: it's now an automatic list-server, not a human being.) To unsubscribe, send to rosegarden-unsubscribe@lists.bath.ac.uk.

You can also browse the mailing-list archive on the Web. Alternatively, the list server can provide copies of past messages from its archives on request by email. You can get details of how this works, and of other list administration commands, by sending to rosegarden-help@lists.bath.ac.uk.
