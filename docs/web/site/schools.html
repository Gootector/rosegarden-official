Rosegarden for schools

As Rosegarden is free software (as in beer and as in speech) it is intended that it it should be available and accessible to the widest range of users as possible. The Rosegarden team is more than happy to give demos to schools or any organisations with an interest in music production. At the moment this service is limited to the areas in and surrounding London, UK but we're hoping to expand this service soon. Please get in touch with us if you want to take advantage of this service or want to talk to us further about Rosegarden and making music under Linux.

Feature requests

As part of our ongoing open development process we're usually able to react quickly to feature requests. If your organisation has a special need or requirement for use or deployment of our software then please contact us.
